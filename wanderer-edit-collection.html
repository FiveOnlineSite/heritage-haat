Why only 12?

We don't list everything we have. We list what we believe in. These 12 designs were handpicked from our Kutch artisan collective — the ones that stopped us mid-selection and made us say yes immediately.

2
Why Goa?

Because these bags belong in the world. On a beach at golden hour, carried by a woman who doesn't overthink her style. Goa was the only honest backdrop — free, warm, and unapologetically itself.

3
Why no restock?

Handcrafted means the artisan moved on. The thread lot changed. The mirror pieces came from a different batch. Your bag is already slightly unique. A restock would just be an imitation of it.

The Wanderer Promise

Your bag arrives
slightly different.
That's the point.

Every piece in The Wanderer Edit is made by hand in Kutch, Gujarat. The artisans who made them have been embroidering since they were children, taught by their mothers, who were taught by theirs.

When something is made by human hands, it carries human variation. The bead that's placed a millimetre left. The thread that ran a shade warmer. The fringe that falls differently on your piece than on the one in the photo.

"The bag you receive may differ slightly from the photo — a bead placed differently, a fringe that falls its own way. Consider it your edition."

This is not a flaw we apologise for. It is the whole reason these bags exist.

"Each stitch is placed by a hand that has placed ten thousand stitches before. The variation is the memory of those hands."

— Heritage Haat, on craft

"No two exactly alike. No restocks. No apologies."

— The Wanderer Edit
