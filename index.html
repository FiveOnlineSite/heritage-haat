Our story

Not just a bag.
A living tradition.

Heritage Haat was born from a simple belief: India's most beautiful things are made by hand, in villages most people never visit. We go there, so you don't have to.

We work directly with artisan families in Kutch — the embroidery capital of the world — ensuring every piece is fairly priced, authentically made, and arrives at your door with its story intact.

"Each stitch takes years to learn. When someone carries our bag in Mumbai, it is like our hands travelling with them."

— Raibahen Sodha, Kutchi Embroidery Artisan

The process

From desert village
to your doorstep

Every Heritage Haat piece travels a remarkable journey before it reaches you.

1
Sourced in Kutch

We visit artisan collectives in Bhuj and surrounding villages to select the finest handcrafted pieces.

2
Quality Checked

Each piece is inspected for embroidery integrity, material quality, and structural durability.

3
Photographed & Listed

Styled with real women, photographed honestly — what you see is what arrives.

4
Delivered with Care

Shipped within 24–48 hours in Mumbai. COD available. Easy returns, no questions asked.

Authentically Handmade

No factory replicas. Every piece is made by a named artisan family in Kutch using traditional techniques passed down through generations.

Fair Artisan Pricing

We pay artisans above market rates and share their names with you. Your purchase directly funds a family's livelihood in rural Kutch.

Free Returns, Always

Not what you expected? Return it within 7 days for a full refund or exchange. No hoops. We want you to love what you carry.
